Temperature vs. Latitude

In this figure, cities' temperature in fahrenheit was plotted against their latitude. This plot shows a curvilinear association between latitude and temperature. As expected, cities closer to the equator were warmer. Those closer to the poles were colder. However, there was greater variability in the northern (positive latitudes) vs. southern (negative latitudes) hemisphere.
